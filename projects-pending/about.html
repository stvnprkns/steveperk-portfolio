Let there be Steve
From Mesquite, TX, where mesquite trees are scarce but Olive Gardens aren't, I grew up where mom stayed at home and Dad fixed cars. Nearly every minute was filled with travelling baseball tournaments. But those few, brief moments of freedom I drew, played video games, and discovered the internet.

Since age 9, I've been hustling—built a landscaping business, cleaned up foreclosures at 13, and rocked retail through high school. College was all about 'ritas and fajitas working in Mexican restaurants, then LA, where I embraced fine dining. Then... a sigh of relief, the ‘in the weeds’ dreams phase of my life was over and began the phase of constantly checking of my posture.
