The Approach

Since we had a lot of work to do in a very short time period, we needed to get these instructions to customers quickly, we created a 3 phase plan.
‚Äç
1) Focus on creating a basic installation guide for the main panel and for the other devices. The MVP of this is to extract the instructions from the manufacturer PDFs and try to make them consistent with each other.
This was difficult because the PDFs are all totally different; some are 52 pages with no pictures, while others are just images.

2) Create Companion content to the walkthrough. Utilize short videos that installers shoot at home, walking through the process of installation.

3) Create a full step-by-step guide in AAA's tone and voice with custom illustrations and high quality videos walking the customer through the installation without the PDF.

Developing the MVP

Since we only had 2 weeks to push the initial MVP to market we had to find some quick ways to get the site up and running.

Firstly, it was going to take too much time to integrate the site into the current AAA architecture so we built a stand alone site that mocked the AAA site.

Guide and Layout

The focus of the guide is to use the best content we have for a given device. For the Control Panel we have full branded content, for others video and a PDF, others, just a PDF.

So we need to layout these pages to emphasize what we have and make it a consistent experience across the devices until they're all rewritten in AAA's voice.
