The Work

Concept Wires

In the initial concepting phase, we aimed to address three critical document management needs: document storage, workflow organization, and template creation. Through meticulous planning, customer journey mapping, data mapping, and iterative sketching, our team embarked on a transformative journey to bring this vision to life.

Documents¬†View

EY's vast repository of client profiles housed an overwhelming number of multi-page documents. Prior to our intervention, these documents existed in a chaotic state, making it challenging for users to navigate through unsorted files. Our primary challenge was to create a user-friendly Document View, equipped with robust sorting functionality and filters, empowering users to effortlessly traverse this vast ocean of data.

Document Templates

Hidden within the seemingly mundane concept of templates lies a truly remarkable feature. Our team, in collaboration with EY and Kunai, embarked on a unified effort to transform the wealth of data collected from thousands of forms into intuitive templates. These templates serve as beacons of efficiency, allowing the A.I. to compare data points across documents automatically. By eliminating the need for manual comparison, users can focus on more meaningful tasks, liberated from mundane data analysis.
