Our team built the mobile application and had a beta group of 50 accountants using it for the 2014 tax season. The app had 4.5 stars on the Apple App Store.

After the success of the mobile application, Intuit made huge changes to the web application, Intuit Tax Online, to create a more mobile-friendly application.

Eventually, the mobile app was absorbed into the web application, using a lot of our learnings from creating a mobile and responsive tax application. Intuit changed the name of the Intuit Tax Online product to Intuit ProConnect shortly after.
